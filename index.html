Why should you stan VICTON?

this is going to be slightly biased.

  • You can see/hear how much they've grown and improved through their music.
  • THEY HAVE AN AMAZING DISCOGRAPHY!!!!
  • You can see hard they've worked to reach where they've come.
  • Every comeback is GOLD.

VICTON also has their own 'signature' sound and distinct voices which differentiates them from other groups.
